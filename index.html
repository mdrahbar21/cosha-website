About us
The Council of Students' for Hostel Affairs (CoSHA) supervises and coordinates student matter and opinion pertaining to hostel administration for all the halls of residence of the institute, and provides a bridge between the institute and the general body of students gymkhana.The CoSHA advises the students senate in matters related to hostel affairs and provides a platform for expression of student opinion on matters related to hostels.
